About YECE School

Our History

Founded in 2017, YECE has been a beacon of educational excellence for over 7 years. Started with just 6 students who trusted in us now serving for Pre-School to Grade 6 annually.

Throughout our journey, we have maintained our commitment to providing quality education while adapting to the changing needs of our students and the evolving educational landscape.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional education that empowers students to become lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and responsible global citizens through innovative teaching methods and a nurturing learning environment.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading educational institution that sets the standard for academic excellence, character development, and innovative learning practices.
